Our Mission

We aim to nurture discipline, strength, and respect through the art of Taekwondo. Our experienced instructors guide students on both physical and mental development, instilling core values like integrity, perseverance, and self-control.

  • Foster a supportive community where students learn to challenge and encourage each other.
  • Build confidence through effective self-defense skills and enhanced physical fitness.
  • Promote mental resilience and a positive attitude toward personal and athletic growth.
  • Develop leadership and teamwork skills, both inside and outside the dojang.
  • Offer competitive training, emphasizing sportsmanship and goal-setting.

Why Join Us?

Experienced Coaches

Our seasoned instructors provide personalized guidance to help you achieve your goals, whether you're a beginner or advanced student.

Flexible Training Schedules

We offer training options that fit into your busy school schedule, with a focus on fitness, self-defense, or competitive excellence.

Local and National Competitions

Our students have opportunities to compete locally and nationally, building confidence and sportsmanship through real-world experiences.

A Supportive Community

Join a welcoming community that fosters respect, friendship, and teamwork, where students support each other on their Taekwondo journey.

Our Sports Program

Varsity Team

Our varsity team consists of advanced students who represent HAU in local and national competitions. Selection is based on skill, dedication, and training performance.

Undertraining Students

For students still developing their skills, we offer targeted training focused on improving technique, fitness, and mental resilience, with the goal of progressing to varsity level.

Who Can Join?

Our HAU Taekwondo Sports Program is exclusively designed for high school and college students enrolled at Holy Angel University.

Whether you're a beginner or an experienced martial artist, we welcome students from both high school and college levels who are eager to learn, grow, and compete. Please note that the program is only open to HAU students, and participation is not available to the general public or non-HAU individuals.
